Embed demo

This page demonstrates the three ways to put the booking widget on any website. Replace data-url with your deployed booking URL.

1. Inline embed

Drops the booking page into a box on your site.

<div class="booking-inline" data-url="/?type=intro-call"></div>
<script src="/embed.js"></script>

2. Popup button (floating)

A floating "Book a time" button in the corner of the page.

<script src="/embed.js"></script>
<script>
  Booking.initPopupButton({ url: "/?type=intro-call", text: "Book a time" });
</script>

3. Popup from a link

Open the widget in a modal when a link or button is clicked.

<a href="#" class="booking-popup" data-url="/?type=intro-call">Schedule now</a>
<script src="/embed.js"></script>
